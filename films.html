My Top 5 Claymation Films

#1

Coraline

Release Date: February 5, 2009

A young girl discovers a parallel universe where everyone has button eyes, but things take a dark turn.

#2

Fantastic Mr. Fox

Release Date: October 14, 2009

A clever fox tries to outwit three farmers, but his plans go awry as he navigates family and friends.

#3

Wallace & Gromit: The Curse of the Were-Rabbit

Release Date: September 4, 2005

Wallace and his dog Gromit must save their village from a monstrous were-rabbit that threatens the harvest festival.

#4

The Boxtrolls

Release Date: August 31, 2014

In a town where evil villains are on a mission to wipe out the Boxtrolls, a young boy raised by these creatures must fight to save them from the evil forces.

#5

Flushed Away

Release Date: October 22, 2006

Roddy, a pet rat, is flushed down the toilet and discovers a whole new world beneath the city streets. He teams up with Rita, a street-smart rat, to find his way back home.
