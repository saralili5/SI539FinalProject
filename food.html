My Top 5 Ann Arbor Foods

I have lived in Ann Arbor now for over seven years. In that time I have tried almost all of the local restaurants and food trucks. Below you will find a collection of rated food items that I consider the best Ann Arbor has to offer in terms of good eats. As my mother always says, don't knock it until you try it!

#1

Ethopian Vegetarian Feast

Restaurant: The Blue Nile, Ann Arbor

This plate has seven traditional ethiopian vegetarian dishes: Gomen, Tekil Gomen, Mixed Vegetables, Metin Shiro Wat, Kik Alecha, Yemisir Kik Wat, Defen Yemisir Alecha. A hidden gem and must-try!

#2

Frita

Restaurant: Frita Batidos, Ann Arbor

A mouth-watering burger made with your choice of protein (my favorite is black bean) served with shoestring fries on top in a soft egg bun. Pro-tip: always make it "LOCO".

#3

Bao Mi

Restaurant: Bao Boys, Ann Arbor

A soft pillowy bun filled with marinated seared tofu, daikon carrot slaw, cilanto, fresh jalepeno, and spicy mayo.

#4

Beef Shawarma

Restaurant: Shawarma Shop, Ann Arbor

This shawarma shop is attached to a gas station but don't let that deter you. The #2 steals the show but don't wait to grab yours because there is always a line at lunch time.

#5

Quesos y Charcuteria

Restaurant: Aventura, Ann Arbor

A board of three cheeses and three meats, housemade pickles, peppers, and jams. Coming from a regular cheese eater - this cheese board may very well be the best in Ann Arbor.

Sara Lili
